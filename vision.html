Mission, Vision and Objectives

Mission

To create creative and self-reliant society.

Vision

Identification Management and mobilization of community resources to uplift the life standard of the marginalized group by establishing good governance and empowering the institutional capabilities.

Objectives

  • Determining the geographical area of operation of the organization.
  • Identifying thematic questions.
  • Identifying and describing the targeted groups.
  • Determining and managing the internal structure of the organization.
  • Determining the organization's vision, mission, goals and objectives.
  • Determining the structure of the organization's policy and working style.
  • Establishing the organization's values, questioning approach and stance.
