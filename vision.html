Our Vision, Mission & Objectives
Vision:

To create creative and self-reliant society



Mission:

Identification Management and mobilization of community resources to uplift the life standard of the marginalized group by establishing good governance and empowering the institutional capabilities



Objectives:
  1. Determining the geographical area of operation of the organization.
  2. Identifying thematic questions.
  3. Identifying and describing the targeted groups.
  4. Determining and managing the internal structure of the organization.
  5. Determining the organization's vision, mission, goals and objectives.
  6. Determining the structure of the organization's policy and working style.
  7. Establishing the organization's values, questioning approach and stance.
