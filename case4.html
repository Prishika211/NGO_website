Tricycle: A convenient instrument making life of Rajkishor easier

Bardaghat Susta (West), Nawalparasi, Palhinandan village municipality, ward no. 1, Rampurwa, Rokapur located Raj Kishor Chaudhary living with his brothers and sisters with a total of 4 people has a good life. He got married with a woman of the same village municipality whose mental state of mind was not healthy and left him. He is living with his younger brother as his elder brother got separated and his sister got married. Five years ago, due to poverty he went to search for job. On the way, he got into an accident near Jhuma Parasi and got his leg fracture. He went under operation and after some time he got fit and fine again. He again went for his work to get thef steel off his leg. He used to work as a labour and even had to beg.

One day, he went on a fair at Rupandehi, Silwatiya and got into a quarrel with an unknown person. He got hard bitten on the quarrel and got harmed on his leg which he went under operation. Due to poverty, he can’t get his treatment done properly and had to rely on others for his living. He used to crawl and was a bed patient.

One day during our program for disabled people on Rokapur, we got notified about him. And after the discussion with ward Chairperson and the all the staffs in our office, we decided him to support a tri cycle from our budget of disabled people. Today, with the support of tri cycle from Indreni Social Development Forum; Raj Kishor is free to move anywhere without relying on others. He wanders a lot and is very happy to meet all his friends with help of this cycle. He can now move from one place to another. He says, “I don’t have to rely on others for a bottle of water. I can now go and drink it myself”. His younger brother says, “In the earlier days, he was unable to do any of the basic tasks but now with the help of this tricycle he does his works himself and everyone is so relieved.”
